📖 How to Play

🎮 Game Controls

  • Use arrow keys ← → ↑ ↓ to move your farmer
  • Collect crops faster than the Robot Farmer!
  • Avoid the brown scarecrows - they block your path
  • Press P to pause/resume the game
  • Reach the goal points to advance to the next level!

🌾 Crop Values

🌾 Wheat = 1 point
🎃 Pumpkin = 3 points
🍎 Golden Apple = 5 points

⚡ Power-Ups & Tips

  • Lightning bolt ⚡ = Double speed for 5 seconds!
  • The blue Robot Farmer moves slowly but smartly
  • You are the green farmer - faster but needs your control!
  • Beat all 3 levels to become the Harvest Champion!
  • Each level gets harder with more scarecrows!

🏆 How to Win

  • Collect crops to earn points before the robot does
  • First to reach the goal points wins the level!
  • If the robot reaches the goal first, game over!
  • Complete all 3 levels to win the entire game!
  • Scores reset each level for fair competition!
